Fire needs fuel, oxygen and heat to start burning.
- Fuel is something that can burn: wood, coal, gas, diesel, wax, cotton.
- Where can we find fire? Wildfire, bonfire, gas lamp, stove.
- Fire is dangerous. Small children are not allowed to play with it.

Fire is bright. It is warm from a distance. It is hot when you are close. Don't stay close or you'll catch on fire.

How can grown-ups start a fire?
- Strike a match.
- Light it from another fire.
- Lightning can strike a tree and start a fire.
- We can use a lighter.
How can a fire be put out?
- It can run out of fuel.
- It stops without oxygen.
- We can spray burning fuel with water.
- Grown-ups can use a fire extinguisher.
